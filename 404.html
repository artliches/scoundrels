identity

From the filthy streets of Doskvol, Kamelin Skelkallan ( He/Her ) aka Junker approaches.

looks

He appears to be vain and concealed. He hails from Severos, and he has palid skin and a curvacious build. His most distinguishing feature is his obvious piercings.
He is currently sporting a tricorn hat fitted loosely.

profession, goals, & methods

Kamelin is gainfully employed as a Dyer and yearns to fix the inequity of Doskvol. He often resorts to long and grueling hours at his job in order to further that goal.

intersts & quirks

During his downtime he often enjoys teaching crows how to commit minor crimes. A quirk that sets him apart from the rest of the people of Doskvol is his obsession with the opinions of his peers.
