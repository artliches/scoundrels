identity

From the filthy streets of Doskvol, Vey Brogan ( She/Them ) aka Skinner approaches.

looks

She appears to be enthusiastic and ambiguous. She hails from Iruvia, and she has dark skin and a muscular build. Her most distinguishing feature is her prosthetic eye.
She is currently sporting a pair of tall boots laced with precision.

profession, goals, & methods

Vey is gainfully employed as a Baker and yearns to be free from her debts. She often resorts to long and complex strategy in order to further that goal.

intersts & quirks

During her downtime she often enjoys dancing with a crowd at a dancehall. A quirk that sets her apart from the rest of the people of Doskvol is her immaculate reputation.
