identity

Drawing a blade with ill-intent, Sesereth Kinclaith ( They/Him ) aka Tick-Tock approaches.

looks

They appear to be cavalier and ambiguous. They hail from Tycheros, and they have umber skin and a broad-shouldered build. Their most distinguishing features are their missing leg and their jagged, glowing lightning shaped horns.
They are currently sporting a tricorn hat fitted loosely.

profession, goals, & methods

Sesereth is gainfully employed as a Chandler and yearns to find someone to love and to be loved. They often resort to obsessively studying their opponents and blackmailing them in order to further that goal.

intersts & quirks

During their downtime they often enjoy being part of a choir at their local church. A quirk that sets them apart from the rest of the people of Doskvol is the possessor spirit that open admits to controlling them.
