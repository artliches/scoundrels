identity

From the filthy streets of Doskvol, Zepar Breakiron ( He/Them ) aka Twelves approaches.

looks

He appears to be a paranoid man. He hails from Iruvia, and he has fair skin and a lithe build. His most distinguishing feature is the golden filigree racing across his body.
He is currently sporting a thick greatcoat britsling with the promise of violence.

profession, goals, & methods

Zepar is gainfully employed as a Jailer and yearns to right the wrongs of this world. He often resorts to long and grueling hours at his job in order to further that goal.

intersts & quirks

During his downtime he often enjoys dressing to the nines and attendning fancy soirees. A quirk that sets him apart from the rest of the people of Doskvol is that his current position is only to spy for a rival faction.
