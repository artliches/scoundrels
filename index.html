identity

Hand on a hidden pistol, Adric Fynfier ( They/Her ) aka Pool approaches.

looks

They appear to be careless and ambiguous. They hail from Iruvia, and they have dark skin and a curvacious build. Their most distinguishing feature is their colorful tattoos.
They are currently sporting a set of suspenders, with one of them hanging off the shoulder.

profession, goals, & methods

Adric is gainfully employed as an Artist and yearns for slow and brutal vengeance. They often resort to bloodless theivery in order to further that goal.

intersts & quirks

During their downtime they often enjoy studying philosophy at Doskvol Academy. A quirk that sets them apart from the rest of the people of Doskvol is their crushing debt to criminals.
